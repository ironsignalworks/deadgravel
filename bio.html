Rust, Ruin & Rock'n'Roll

DEAD GRAVEL drags rock ’n’ roll through the dirt and makes it holy again. It’s sweat, smoke, salvation, and a prayer you won’t remember in the morning.

“Ruin My Fun,” the opening track from the demo Dustborn, kicks down the door with a sneer and a smile. Loud, loose, and unapologetically alive.

Line-Up:
Joey Del Grave — Vocals, Harmonica
Layla Stone — Keyboards, Vocals
Jett Nash — Guitars
Dusty Reyes — Guitar
Sloane Vega — Bass
Victor Silver — Drums

A six-piece storm of desert thunder and dive-bar soul, DEAD GRAVEL blends swamp and swagger into something born to be blasted through busted speakers.

Dustborn is the sound of six outlaws with nothing to lose, howling down the highway on bald tires and borrowed time. With nods to Fleetwood Mac’s harmony, Blue Öyster Cult’s mystique, The Stooges’ danger, and The New York Dolls’ revved-up swagger, it’s rock ’n’ roll stripped to bones, blood, and smoke.

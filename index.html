Debut Album in the Works:
Voodoo County

Dead Gravel are currently working on their first album, Voodoo County – a 12-track southern gothic road record steeped in grit, and gasoline.

From backwoods sermons to desert ghost towns, it drags the listener through the lowlands of sin and salvation, told through rusted guitars, slow-burn grooves, and voices that sound half-dead, half-divine.
